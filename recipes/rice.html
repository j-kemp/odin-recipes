Buttered Cilantro Lime Rice

Having a hard time to decide on what side to pair with your meat?

Rice. But that's too easy, so let's zest it up a little...

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Thouroughly rinse the rice
  2. Place rice in rice cooker with enough water to reach the first knuckle on your pointer finger
  3. Follow the steps on your rice cooker
  4. When done add butter to the rice cooker and close lid to melt
  5. Zest Lime then add it to the rice
  6. Add cilantro to rice
  7. Squeeze lime juice into rice, to taste
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste
  9. Serve
