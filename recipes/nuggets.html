Chicken Nuggets

What is there to say?

You love them, I love them, the kids love them. So lets get to it...

Ingredients

For Marinade

  • Boneless chicken
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Baking powder
  • Pickle juice

For Frying

  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Frying oil

Steps

The Prep

  1. Rinse and dry chicken
  2. Cut chicken into desired nugget size
  3. Place chicken in container with all ingredients for marinade
  4. Marinade for at least 1 hour
  5. combine 50/50 cornstarch and flour
  6. Add dry mixture to marinade/chicken

Lets Get Fried

  1. Pre-heat frying oil to 350
  2. Add marinated chicken in batches to prevent oil from dropping temp to much
  3. Cook nugets for 3-5 minutes, agitating ocassionaly to keep them from sticking. Rule of thumb is if they are floating they are most likely done.
  4. Place completed nuggets on wire rack to cool, not a paper towel or they will not stay crispy
  5. Serve with side(s) of choice and dip of choice
